MASTER PLAN

Purpose of Master Plan

The Purpose of the Master Plan is to establish a framework to guide the physical development of UNHAS-FE Gowa campus including the future extension. The quality of education provided in a university campus is in large part affected by the quality of the facilities where pedagogical activities are conducted.

The campus master plan will ensure that the design and future development will be in compliance with UNHAS’s academic strategic plan.
